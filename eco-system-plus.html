Public Safety Mission

Our ultimate goal is to help individual first responders, supervisors, and department heads detect and prevent stress from harming both the individual responders as well as the community members they serve.

Research has shown that police officers who can control their physiology will their do better during stressful scenarios and are more likely to be protected against the short- and long-term risks of PTSD.

Code4Pro empowers you with the data to transform the health of public safety in your community.
