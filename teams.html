Team Overview

How we started...

Our company is the result of a dire community need to help police officers better manage stress in one of the world’s most dangerous jobs.

Now we’ve realized that other high value, high stress occupations can also benefit from the solution.

One of our founders, a retired chief of police, relayed the story of a police officer’s wife who said the constant stress from facing the terrors of daily incidents had turned her husband into someone she doesn’t know anymore. At his retirement party, she said something has to be done for him and his fellow officers, because she was afraid his retirement will be a short-lived period of torment.

So we assembled a group of data scientists, technologists and software engineers to find a solution, using the latest developments in biometrics and artificial intelligence.

The result is Code4Pro.
