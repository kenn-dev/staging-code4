Nestor for Tactical Operators

High-stakes professionals—including military operators, law enforcement, and tactical teams—face relentless cognitive and emotional stress in the line of duty. Over time, this sustained load can erode performance, suppress emotion, degrade decision-making, and in some cases, lead to long-term conditions such as burnout or PTSD.

With the integration of AI-driven HRV analytics, a new frontier has emerged: the ability to achieve cognitive overmatch at the edge. By gaining real-time insight into physiological readiness and stress states, tactical professionals can make faster, more informed decisions—and control the situation before it controls them.
